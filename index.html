St. Bernard Dogs

The St. Bernard is a large working dog from the Italy and Switzerland. The dog was first bred to work at a hospice in the Great St. Bernard Pass. It is recognized as one of the molosser breeds. Molosser breed are a breed of dogs with related ancestry that generally are large and used for protection. The name originates from the now extinct Molossus dog from ancient Greece.

It is a theory that all mastiff breeds descend from the Molossus. The Molossian Hound was known through out ancient Greece for being large and ferocious. Their is a well known story that Alexander the Great found the original Molosser in Asia and gifted it to the Molossi Pricnes's son and all mastif breeds in the west originate from this dog.
